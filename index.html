Prototype-Based Multiple Video Synchronization Application

Our Multiple Videos Synchronization application enables seamless synchronization of multiple videos without relying on a reference video. By identifying the common alignment among videos within the same action class, we can effortlessly warp each video to its respective prototype. This eliminates the need for selecting a reference video, which can be challenging for extensive datasets. With our application, achieving synchronization across multiple videos becomes more accurate and efficient process.
